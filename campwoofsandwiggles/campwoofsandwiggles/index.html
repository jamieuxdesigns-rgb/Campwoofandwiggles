Welcome to Camp Woofs and Wiggles!

Where wagging tails and happy paws are our top priority.
At Camp Woofs and Wiggles, we believe every pup deserves a place to play, learn, and relax in a safe, loving environment. Whether it’s Day Camp, Boarding, Training, or Grooming, we treat every dog like family.

Our services are designed to support your dog’s health, happiness, and tail-wagging excitement. Let us take the lead while you're away—book today!

Day Camp

From playtime in the sun to cozy nap breaks, our day camp is packed with enrichment and care. Full-day pups enjoy snacks, meds if needed, and one-on-one attention from our team.

Boarding

Sleepovers at Camp Woofs and Wiggles include playtime perks, cozy bunks with pup-cams for check-ins, and a spa day for guests staying three nights or more!
